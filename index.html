Step 1 — set target days of supply per channel
How to read this table: Set your target Days of Supply (DOS) per channel using the sliders above. The engine calculates how many units each channel needs, then checks if WH 240 can cover all non-Amazon channels. Amazon US and CA have their own FBA inventory pool (available + inbound) separate from WH 240. The WH 240 surplus column shows units remaining in WH 240 after all Shopify, Chewy, Walmart, TikTok, and Target+ allocations are fully met. A green surplus means you have buffer stock. A "—" means inventory is tight and being fair-shared across channels.
